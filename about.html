The Story Behind the Strategy

Born and raised in Ecuador, I discovered early on that discipline and determination could take you anywhere. As a competitive triathlete, I learned that success isn't just about talent—it's about showing up consistently, pushing through challenges, and staying true to your goals even when the path gets difficult.

When my family's business collapsed during my teenage years, I experienced firsthand how quickly life can change. But instead of breaking me, this challenge taught me resilience and the importance of building something meaningful from the ground up. It's this experience that drives my passion for helping entrepreneurs create sustainable, purpose-driven businesses.

My international career began with managing over 500 YouTube channels, where I learned that authentic storytelling and strategic thinking could create incredible impact. From launching campaigns for global brands like Estee Lauder and Aveda to working with creators across Latin America, I've seen how powerful marketing can be when it's rooted in genuine purpose.

Now based in Lisbon, Portugal, I work with conscious entrepreneurs who want to grow their brands without compromising their values. My approach combines data-driven strategy with human-centered storytelling, ensuring that every campaign not only drives results but also creates meaningful connections with your audience.
