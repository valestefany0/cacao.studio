About

I'm Valeria Ocaña, a digital marketing strategist focused on helping founders, creators, and mission-led brands communicate with more clarity, relevance, and intention.

My work sits between strategy and storytelling. I care about what makes a brand grow, but also about what makes it trusted, memorable, and genuinely aligned.

Over the years, I've worked across creator ecosystems, digital campaigns, and brand growth initiatives, supporting content strategy, messaging, audience development, and communication that connects both emotionally and commercially.

I'm especially drawn to projects that have substance — businesses and people with something real to say, sell, or build — and that want to do it in a way that feels intelligent, human, and sustainable.

What matters to me is not just helping brands be seen. It is helping them be understood.

Outside of metrics and strategy, I believe the strongest brands are built through clarity, consistency, and emotional truth. *
